Your rental data is your leverage

As a property manager, you work hard to satisfy your tenants and property owners. When you take ownership of the daily operations, it’s easy to justify the management fees you charge. For propety owners, this may not always be clear.

Resident Metrics helps property management companies close more leads by tracking metrics that are important to property owners. It’s time to start making the data that you collect, work for you.

Move beyond standard services

The property management services that you offer are often similar to those of your competition. Whether or not you close another lead is therefore, largely based on the the price of those services. With Resident Metrics you can move beyond standard services like “online payment collection” and “tenant screening”, and start providing rental metrics that prove you know how to manage your properties. When you keep track of critical metrics, you can show property owners that they will be making (or will continue to be make) a good investment by choosing you, over your competitors.

Features

Track Maintenance Fees

The amount of monthly maintenance fees collected, determines your maintenance fees to collected rent ratio. When this percentage is high, it means a large portion of your revenue comes from maintenance fees. When low, it means lower costs and more money for property owners.

Compare Rental Prices

Educate and partner with your customers by providing them insight on rent trends in various locations. Track the number of new rentals every month as well as how many renewals were at, above, or below the previous rental price, compared to other local properties.

Reduce Holding Costs

Calculate the average length of stay on the properties that you manage. This metric can be used internally to measure the quality of your tenant services. It can also be valuable to property owners because it allows them to anticipate the future performance of their investment.

Drill down into Vacancies

Find out exactly how many of your vacancies have reached the end of their lease and how many of those included extensions. You can also see how many tenants broke out of their lease on a month by month basis.

Generate Reports

Easily create and share reports when you need them. Alternatively, create a task and have them generated and distributed every month. Do this, and you'll create a stronger relationship with your customers and promote future business.

Available Anywhere

You no longer need to rush back to the office to send a potential customer the spreadsheet with the data they are looking for. Whether your are in the office or on your mobile device, you can access all of your metrics, right from where you are.
